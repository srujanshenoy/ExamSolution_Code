### 1. The difference b/w the smallest +ve and greatest -ve integer is __ {collapsible="true" default-state="expanded"}
+ (a) 2
+ (b) -2
+ (c) 1
+ (d) -1

$largest negative = -1$ <br/>
$difference = 1 -(-1) = 2(a)$
